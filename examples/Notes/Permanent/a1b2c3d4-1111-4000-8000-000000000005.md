Evergreen notes are continuously revised and updated, growing richer over time. Unlike static reference material, they evolve as understanding deepens.

## Characteristics

- Written in your own words
- Densely linked to other notes
- Atomic: one concept per note
- Updated as thinking evolves

This concept aligns closely with the permanent notes in the [[a1b2c3d4-1111-4000-8000-000000000001|Zettelkasten Method]].
