A Zettelkasten can be modeled as a directed graph where notes are nodes and links are edges. This perspective reveals structural properties of the knowledge base.

## Key Metrics

- **Degree centrality**: Notes with many links are hubs
- **Orphan notes**: Nodes with zero edges (disconnected)
- **Clusters**: Groups of densely interconnected notes

Understanding graph structure helps identify gaps and opportunities for new connections.
