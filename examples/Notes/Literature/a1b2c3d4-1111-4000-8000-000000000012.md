## Source

Waitzkin, J. (2007). The Art of Learning. Free Press.

## Key Ideas

- Mastery comes from internalizing fundamentals, not memorizing surface patterns
- "Making smaller circles": deepening understanding of core concepts
- The learning process itself can be learned and refined

## Notes

Waitzkin's emphasis on depth over breadth connects to the idea of [[a1b2c3d4-1111-4000-8000-000000000005|Evergreen Notes]] - iteratively deepening understanding rather than collecting information.
