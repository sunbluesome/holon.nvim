## Source

Ahrens, S. (2017). How to Take Smart Notes. CreateSpace.

## Key Ideas

- Writing is not the outcome of thinking, it IS the thinking process
- The slip-box (Zettelkasten) externalizes memory and creates a dialogue partner
- Bottom-up organization emerges naturally from linking

## Notes

This book introduced me to the [[a1b2c3d4-1111-4000-8000-000000000001|Zettelkasten Method]]. The core insight is that note-taking should be an active process of reformulation, not passive copying.

The concept of [[a1b2c3d4-1111-4000-8000-000000000005|Evergreen Notes]] is strongly influenced by Ahrens' description of permanent notes.
